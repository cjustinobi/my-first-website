The Redeemed Christian Corpers Fellowship RCCF is an arm of Christ Redeemers Ministries CRM and extension of the Redeemed Christian Fellowships RCF; you had a good time with the Lord while on campus as an RCFite and you wish to continue in the same spirit in your National Service Year, this is the right platform for you to do that.

The Fellowship is dedicated primarily to a life of Devotion, Service to God and Humanity through various outreaches and community service programs. These include rural outreaches, prison and orphanage programs. We are also committed to raising up generation of godly men and women who understand the meaning of a life of sacrifice and leadership, by imparting them with life changing principles.

God Bless you as you plan to fellowship with us, new members are also welcome.
