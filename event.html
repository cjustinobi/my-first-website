The School of Disciple.

The School of Disciples SOD, An arm of Christ The Redeemers Ministries which started in 1985, cordinated by The Training Bureau of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, having Pastor E.A. Adeboye as the Principal, has chosen RCCF, Eket as one of her centers.

SOD is a school where Christians of all denominations learn how to be true and genuine Disciples of Jesus Christ and how to study to show oneself approved unto God.

Considering the Intending new members RCCF, and to encourage the RCCFite at large, The School has agreed to reduce the duration of the Programme from a period 12months to 7months. Also, the School fees, which stood at the rate of #21,500, has also been slashed to #8,500

Corps Members in the House Expressed their delight as majority enrolled in the school.
