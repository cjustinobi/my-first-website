Who We Are

Redeemed Christian Corpers' Fellowship, RCCF, is an arm of Christ The Redeemers' Ministry with National Headquarters at No.1-7 Redemption way Ebutte Metta, Lagos, having Pastor E.A. Adeboye as President.

It is a Sister Fellowship to the local Redeemed Christian Fellowship (RCF) and Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG).

What We Do

  1. Evangelizing the suburbs of the country(states of posting).

  2. Planting fellowship centers/churches in every local goverment area.

  3. Caring for welfare of our corps members from various states to their states of national service.

  4. Making sure that every corps member who is a member of RCCF/CRM feels at home and participates actively in the mission in the state.

  5. Establishment of RCF in higher institution of learning where it is not in existence.

  6. Having a secretariat/accomodation (family house) to cater for the newly posted corps member in their state of posting before securing a permanent place of abode, etc

  7. Organizing rallies, symposia, mission outreaches, social and welfare outreach, lectures' crusades for the propagation of the gospel of Christ. Bible studies, talks, educational outreach, drama, seminars, any other programmes.
